Add your audio files

Click "Add Audio Files" or drag & drop MP3 / OGG files into the drop zone. Multiple files will auto-fill empty slots in order.

2

Assign tracks to slots

Each slot maps to a specific PES 17 in-game sound. Click "Choose" next to any slot to assign a specific track. Drag rows to swap tracks between slots.

3

Preview your tracks

Use the play button (▶) to preview any assigned track right in the browser before you finalize.

4

Export & pack

Click "Export CPK Manifest" to download a music_cpk_manifest.txt with renaming instructions and folder paths.

File naming: PES 17 expects .adx filenames inside the CPK. Rename your audio files as shown in the manifest, place them in Asset/Audio/BGM/ and use CPK Tools to pack them into music.cpk, then place that file in PES17/Data/.

Slot categories: The builder covers Menu & UI (main menu, edit, matchday screens), Match Atmosphere (kick-off, half-time, goal jingles, FT results), Anthems (UCL, UEL, and 4 club anthem slots), Commentary, and Highlights — 28 slots total.
