How it works

1
Upload your MP3s

Drag files onto the page or click "Add MP3 Files". You can add multiple files at once — they'll auto-fill empty slots.

2
Assign tracks to slots

Each slot maps to a specific PES 17 in-game audio cue — menus, anthems, goal jingles, highlights, and more. Click "Choose" to pick a file for a specific slot, or preview with ▶.

3
Download the ZIP

Click Download ZIP. The ZIP contains your files already renamed and placed inside the correct folder path: Asset/Audio/BGM/.

4
Pack with CPK Tools

Open CPK Tools, point it at the extracted ZIP folder, and pack it into music.cpk. Drop that file into your PES 2017/Data/ folder. Done.

⚠ File format: PES 17 uses .adx internally, but most CPK tools accept MP3/OGG and handle conversion. Check your CPK tool's docs if audio doesn't play in-game.
